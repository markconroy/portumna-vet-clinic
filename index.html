About Portumna Vet Clinic

Here at Portumna Vet Clinic we aim to provide a quality veterinary service to our community and the greater East Galway and North Tipperary areas.

With a team of four diligent veterinary surgeons and hard working office staff we strive to attend to all our clients veterinary needs. Our services include a wide range of large animal services, routine equine services, daily small animal clinics and weekly times for small animal surgeries.
